Prediction Methods
Understanding how stock prices are forecasted
📈
Technical Analysis
Uses historical price data, volume, moving averages (SMA/EMA), RSI, MACD, and chart patterns to identify trends and forecast future movements.
🏢
Fundamental Analysis
Evaluates a company's financial health — earnings, revenue growth, P/E ratio, and debt levels — to estimate fair value and future price.
🤖
AI & Machine Learning
Models like Random Forest, KNN, and neural networks are trained on historical data to identify complex patterns beyond human detection.
🎯
Analyst Consensus
Wall Street sell-side analysts issue 12-month price targets based on deep sector expertise. TipRanks and WallStreetZen aggregate these signals.
💬
Sentiment Analysis
Scans news, social media, and earnings calls to gauge investor sentiment and its potential impact on future stock price movements.
